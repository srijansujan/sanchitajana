Sanchita hails from the village of Mahishadal in East Midnapore. She lost her father to cancer when she was only seven years old. He used to work as a driver for a small private firm in Haldia. Since then she grew up at her maternal uncle’s place. In 2012, her mother was diagnosed with cancer too. Fighting against all odds, Sanchita cleared her Madhyamik and H.S Examinations and is currently pursuing a bachelor’s in Mathematics from Mahishadal Raj College. She is also passionate about painting and gives drawing lessons to kids at home to support herself. Srijan Sujan has been with Sanchita since 2015.
